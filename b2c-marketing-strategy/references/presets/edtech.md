# Edtech / online learning

**Examples:** Duolingo, Coursera, Udemy, Skillshare, MasterClass

**Typical segments:** K-12 supplemental, university, professional upskillers, language learners, hobby learners (free-tier dominant).

**Channel split benchmark:** Performance 30-40 / Brand 25-35 / Influence 10-15 / CRM 12-18 / Tools+Agencies 5-8 / Reserve 3

**KPI metrics:** Active learners, course completion rate, paid conversion, ARPU, churn, NPS, certificate issuance.

**Category risks:** AI substitution for course creation / grading, free-tier defection, certification credibility erosion, university partnerships disputes.

**Strategically distinctive:** Acquisition-heavy because completion rates are low (single-digit %). CRM lifecycle to surface engagement-based hooks. Free-tier is the main funnel.

**Radar axes (Competitors section):** Course quality · Instructor reputation · Outcomes / certification · Pricing · Engagement (gamification) · Mobile UX

**Brand-funnel benchmark (category leader, Mode 1 hypothesis anchor):** TOM 15–30% · Aided 50–75% · Consideration 25–40% · Trial / paid-conversion 12–25%
