# Subscription streaming

**Examples:** Netflix, Spotify, Disney+, Paramount, Apple TV+

**Typical segments:** Cord-cutters, students (price-sensitive), families, music vs video preference, password-sharers (now monetized cohorts), churners.

**Channel split benchmark:** Performance 25-35 / Brand 25-35 / Influence 10-15 / CRM 15-20 / Tools+Agencies 5-8 / Reserve 5

**KPI metrics:** Paid subscribers, ARPU, churn (monthly / annual), net adds, content engagement hours, cancel-then-return rate.

**Category risks:** Content cost inflation, content licensing churn, ad-tier cannibalization, password-sharing crackdown backlash, family-plan abuse.

**Strategically distinctive:** Brand IS content. Marketing largely promotes specific shows / albums. CRM is unusually heavy because retention = content engagement.

**Radar axes (Competitors section):** Library depth · Originals / exclusives · Discovery · Audio / video quality · Pricing · Cross-device UX

**Brand-funnel benchmark (category leader, Mode 1 hypothesis anchor):** TOM 35–55% (Netflix / Spotify-led) · Aided 85–95% · Consideration 50–65% · Trial / paid-conversion 35–55%
