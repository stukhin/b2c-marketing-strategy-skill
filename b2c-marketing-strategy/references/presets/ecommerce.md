# Full-stack e-commerce

**Examples:** Trendyol, Wildberries, Shein, Mercado Libre, Allegro

**Typical segments:** Frequency-based (daily / weekly / monthly / occasional shoppers), category preference (fashion, electronics, home, FMCG), price-sensitivity (premium / mid / value).

**Channel split benchmark:** Performance 40-50 / Brand 15-20 / Influence 8-12 / CRM 12-18 / Tools+Agencies 5-8 / Reserve 5

**KPI metrics:** GMV, AOV, repeat rate, contribution margin, take rate, cart abandonment, NPS.

**Category risks:** Logistics cost spike, big-tech entry (Amazon expansion), import-tariff regulation, returns abuse, marketplace seller fraud.

**Strategically distinctive:** Logistics IS the product for full-stack. Brand differentiation often via service speed / reliability rather than assortment. CRM has higher % weight than typical B2C.

**Radar axes (Competitors section):** Selection breadth · Price competitiveness · Delivery speed · Return policy · Trust · Mobile UX

**Brand-funnel benchmark (category leader, Mode 1 hypothesis anchor):** TOM 20–30% · Aided 70–85% · Consideration 35–50% · Trial / 90-day-active 25–40%
