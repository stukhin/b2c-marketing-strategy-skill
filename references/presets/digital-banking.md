# Digital banking & neo-wallet

**Examples:** Revolut, N26, Wise (consumer), Monzo, Starling

**Typical segments:** Travelers / FX-savvy, freelancers / SMB owners, Gen Z first-bank, immigrants (remittance), affluent multi-banked.

**Channel split benchmark:** Performance 35-45 / Brand 25-35 / Influence 5-10 / CRM 12-18 / Tools+Agencies 4-8 / Reserve 3

**KPI metrics:** Verified accounts, KYC-cleared accounts, primary-bank %, deposit balance, monthly active card users, FX volume, gross profit per user.

**Category risks:** Regulator licensing changes, KYC/AML compliance cost spike, incumbent retaliation (free X), feature commoditization (everyone now has FX).

**Strategically distinctive:** Trust + brand are core moats — extreme attention on `.hyp` / `.hypothesis-badge` if no real tracker. Compliance and trust copy is unusually heavy in creative.

**Radar axes (Competitors section):** Fee transparency · Speed (transfers / openings) · Trust · Modern UX · Card / product breadth · Customer service

**Brand-funnel benchmark (challenger leader, Mode 1 hypothesis anchor):** TOM 8–18% · Aided 40–65% · Consideration 20–35% · Trial / activated-account 10–25%
