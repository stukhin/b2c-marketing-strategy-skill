# Investment & trading consumer

**Examples:** Robinhood, Trade Republic, eToro, Tinkoff Invest, Stash, Acorns

**Typical segments:** First-time retail investors, active day traders, passive index investors, crypto-curious, high-net-worth (often product-tier).

**Channel split benchmark:** Performance 35-45 / Brand 20-30 / Influence 10-15 / CRM 10-15 / Tools+Agencies 5-8 / Reserve 3

**KPI metrics:** Funded accounts, AUM, trade volume, net deposits, churn, cohort retention, active days per user.

**Category risks:** Regulator retail-investor protection (PFOF, leverage caps), market crash effect on active retail, crypto contagion, influencer compliance scandals.

**Strategically distinctive:** Bull market = explosive growth, bear market = retention crisis. Influence channel high in education + acquisition. Compliance is brand-critical.

**Radar axes (Competitors section):** Fee level · Asset breadth · Research / education tools · Mobile UX · Trust · Onboarding speed

**Brand-funnel benchmark (challenger / category leader, Mode 1 hypothesis anchor):** TOM 8–18% · Aided 35–60% · Consideration 18–35% · Trial / funded-account 8–20%
