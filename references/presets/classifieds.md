# Classifieds marketplace

**Examples:** OLX, Kijiji, Quikr, Marktplaats, eBay Kleinanzeigen

**Typical segments:** Active sellers (high-volume listers), occasional sellers, professional dealers, buyers (in-market vs browsers).

**Channel split benchmark (% of envelope):** Performance 35-45 / Brand 20-25 / Influence 5-10 / CRM 10-15 / Tools+Agencies 5-8 / Reserve 5-10

**KPI metrics:** Active listings, MAU, listing-to-contact rate, repeat seller rate, take-rate (if monetized), category penetration.

**Category risks:** Marketplace winner-take-most dynamics, supply-side fraud, vertical specialists eating share (cars, real estate splitting off), professional sellers cannibalizing C2C trust.

**Strategically distinctive:** Two-sided liquidity is the real product. Brand health usually matters less than supply density. Localised category coverage > broad-but-thin presence.

**Radar axes (Competitors section):** Listing depth · Listing quality · Trust & verification · Search UX · Price transparency · Mobile UX

**Brand-funnel benchmark (category leader, Mode 1 hypothesis anchor):** TOM 30–50% · Aided 75–90% · Consideration 45–60% · Trial / 90-day-active 30–45%
