# Jobs marketplace

**Examples:** LinkedIn, HeadHunter, Indeed, Naukri, JobStreet

**Typical segments:** Active job seekers (passive applicants), career changers, employers (SMB / enterprise / agency), recruiters.

**Channel split benchmark:** Performance 30-40 / Brand 25-30 / Influence 5-10 / CRM 15-20 / Tools+Agencies 5-10 / Reserve 5

**KPI metrics:** Job postings, applications, employer accounts, ATS integrations, time-to-fill, employer NPS.

**Category risks:** LinkedIn dominance in white-collar, AI-CV-spam degrading employer experience, gig platforms cannibalising blue-collar, regulator labor-data rules.

**Strategically distinctive:** Two-sided again, but supply (employers) much higher CAC than demand (job seekers). Brand work skews to employers, performance to seekers.

**Radar axes (Competitors section):** Listing volume · Match quality · Employer trust · Application UX · Salary transparency · Mobile UX

**Brand-funnel benchmark (category leader, Mode 1 hypothesis anchor):** TOM 25–40% (LinkedIn-led white-collar) · Aided 70–85% · Consideration 40–55% · Trial / 90-day-active 25–40%
