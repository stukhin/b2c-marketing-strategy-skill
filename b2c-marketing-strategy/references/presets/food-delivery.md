# Food delivery (restaurants)

**Examples:** Uber Eats, DoorDash, Deliveroo, Glovo, Talabat, Just Eat Takeaway

**Typical segments:** Frequent orderers (3+ / week), occasional, family / household, late-night / weekend, business orderers.

**Channel split benchmark:** Performance 35-45 / Brand 20-25 / Influence 5-10 / CRM 15-20 / Tools+Agencies 5-8 / Reserve 3

**KPI metrics:** Orders, GMV, AOV, restaurant partner count, repeat rate, take rate (12-30%), drivers / hour.

**Category risks:** Restaurant churn (commission disputes), driver supply, economic sensitivity (discretionary spend), in-house delivery from chains.

**Strategically distinctive:** Three-sided (consumer / restaurant / driver). Brand work matters with restaurants (account churn) AS MUCH as consumer. Geo-density rules.

**Radar axes (Competitors section):** Restaurant breadth · Delivery time · Pricing · Restaurant ratings · App UX · Promo intensity

**Brand-funnel benchmark (category leader, Mode 1 hypothesis anchor):** TOM 25–40% · Aided 70–85% · Consideration 40–55% · Trial / 90-day-active 30–45%
